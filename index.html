ABOUT

The Logics, Artificial Intelligence and Formal Methods Lab (LIAMF) has been created in 2000 and consists currently of six staff members and their graduate and undergraduate students. We are involved in research funded by the Brazilian government and/or institutional funding agencies, international agencies as well as industry. We offer regular courses at graduate and undergraduate levels and also short courses on specific topics, usually related to ongoing projects.
We hold weekly meetings where members of the group or their guests can present their ongoing research. The program for the current semester can be obtained here.
